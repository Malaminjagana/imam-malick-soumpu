Information Security Policy

Al-Imam Malik Islamic Institute is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information collected, stored, or processed through our website and internal systems. This policy outlines the principles and practices we follow to ensure secure and responsible handling of information.

1. Data Protection

We take appropriate measures to safeguard personal and institutional data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

2. Secure Website Practices

Our website uses modern security standards, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure hosting, and regular updates to reduce vulnerabilities.

3. User Privacy

Personal information submitted through forms or communication channels is handled with care and used only for legitimate institutional purposes.

4. Access Control

Only authorized staff members have access to sensitive information. Access is granted based on role and responsibility.

5. Data Storage

Information is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for administrative, educational, or legal purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

When using external services (such as hosting or analytics), we ensure they follow industry-standard security practices.

7. Incident Response

In the event of a security breach or suspicious activity, we take immediate action to investigate, contain, and resolve the issue.

8. Policy Updates

This Information Security Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements, new regulations, or changes in technology.
