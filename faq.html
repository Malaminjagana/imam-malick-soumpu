Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What programs does the institute offer?

We offer Tahfiz (Qur’an memorization), Islamic Studies, Arabic language, and academic support programs.

Where is the institute located?

The institute is located in The Gambia and serves students from various regions.

How can I enroll?

Enrollment is done through our admissions office. You can contact us or visit the institute for details.

Do you offer Tahfiz classes?

Yes, Tahfiz is one of our main programs, taught by qualified teachers.

Is the institute open to all ages?

Yes, we accept students of different ages depending on the program.
