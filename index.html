Culture and Knowledge

Why Choose Imam Malick Islamic Institute?

Rooted in the teachings of Imam Malik, the institute offers disciplined Islamic education through its Tahfiz program and Islamic School program, providing strong moral guidance and a learning environment that shapes confident, value‑driven students.

Accredited

Al‑Imam Malick Islamic Institute upholds strong academic and Islamic standards across all levels Elementary, Preparatory, and High School. The institute follows the Maliki school of jurisprudence and maintains a curriculum aligned with national education guidelines in The Gambia, ensuring quality Islamic and academic learning.

Cultural Diversity

The institute welcomes students from across The Gambia and West Africa, creating a vibrant environment enriched with multiple languages, cultures, and shared Islamic values. This diversity strengthens unity, brotherhood, and mutual respect among students.

Scholarships

Al‑Imam Malick Islamic Institute supports families by offering accessible education, including scholarship opportunities for outstanding students and those in need. These scholarships help students pursue Islamic studies and academic excellence without financial barriers.

Location The Gambia

Al‑Imam Malick Islamic Institute is located in a peaceful community within The Gambia, providing students with an environment that supports Islamic learning, discipline, and academic growth. The institute serves families across the region and stands as a center of knowledge rooted in the Maliki tradition.

Mahmadu Kabba Musa

Former director of the Imam Malik Institute and founder of Karate in The Gambia

Mahmadu Musa Kabba: A Legacy of Discipline, Courage, and Service. Mahmadu Musa Kabba, the former director of the institute, was a pioneering figure in The Gambia. He introduced the art of self‑defense known as Karate to the country and registered it legally, laying the foundation for a national discipline that shaped generations. Under his guidance, countless students trained with dedication including politicians, members of the Gambian Armed Forces, and even the President himself. His impact extended far beyond the dojo, influencing character, discipline, and national pride.
May Allah forgive him and grant him Jannah.

Ebrahim Marry Janaga

Director of the Imam Malik Institute and Member of Gambia Islamic Council

Ebrahim Marry Janaga he is the director of the Imam Malik Institute and member of gambia islamic council He has been working in the field of Islamic education for more than 20 years and has contributed to the development of many students and scholars. He is also known for his efforts in promoting interfaith dialogue and understanding among different communities in the Gambia.
